ABOUT NIGERIA FOOD

by Faith Makinde|1 day ago

Nigeria has an incredibly rich and diverse culinary tradition, shaped by its ethnic diversity, geography, and history. Nigerian food is known for its bold flavors, spiciness, and the use of a variety of ingredients like yam, cassava, plantain, rice, beans, and an array of meats and fish.
